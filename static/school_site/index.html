A Message from the Principal

"Welcome to AAPS Yugo, where we nurture potential and inspire excellence..."

On behalf of all Al-Asr Team members I warmly welcome you at Al-Asr School System. We are passionate about offering children an exciting, stimulating and rich curriculum, based on real reasons for learning in a safe, conducive and child friendly environment. At Al-Asr schools, we have a firm belief that every child should have the opportunity to utilize his or her maximum potential and develop the academic, creative, social and spiritual skills resulting in the formation of a capable, creative and competent member of the society. Alhamdulillah, we have a mission to serve our students and community through our efforts. Please take the time to visit our school to see just how exciting Al-Asr School is!

Hamidullah
Principal, AL Asar Public School Yugo
